Add Bucket List

The Bucket List section is where you can dream big and plan out the experiences you want to have in life. It's a chance to think about what brings you joy, what challenges you want to overcome, and what you want to achieve. Start by brainstorming a list of things you've always wanted to do, no matter how big or small. Then, prioritize the items on your list and set goals for when and how you'll achieve them. Remember, life is short, so don't be afraid to dream big and make the most of your time!
