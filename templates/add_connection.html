Relationships.

Reflecting on what a person means to you can help you develop deeper connections with them. Consider what you would like to say to them while they are alive, how often you need to make time for them, and what you would say to them on their birthday or at their funeral. While this can be a heavy exercise, it can help you think more deeply about the person and be more intentional in your interactions with them in the future. Use this exercise as an opportunity to strengthen your relationships with those who matter most to you.
