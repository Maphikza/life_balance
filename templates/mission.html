Our Mission:

We started {{name}} because we felt that the world would be a better place if people were connected to what is important to them. If someone does not know who they are and who they want to be, there is an entire industry dedicated to telling you how to be in a way that helps their profit motives and is not always what is good for society. While technology has made it easier to connect to more people, in many cases we feel that it has taken away deep connections, and we are all paying the price for it as there is so much loneliness in the world.

We believe that by creating this space and providing the tools and guides to help people think about why they are here and what they would like to stand for, we can have more people who are driven by a deep sense of meaning. The idea that having a good "why" can make you put up with almost any "how" rings true to us.

We did not create the tool to be another place where you get to broadcast your ideas and thoughts. We created this tool for individuals to work on themselves with the hope that as they gain a better understanding of who they are, they will become better people and thus be a force for good in the world. We believe that a world full of people who know themselves and have decided how they want to be in this world will start seeing a more connected world, where relationships have deepened, and we believe that society will benefit greatly from this.

We would like {{name}} to be accessible to as many people as possible free of charge. We trust that if our mission is worthwhile, it will have people who support it. If you are in a position where you can support us financially, please consider supporting us here. Your support will be truly appreciated.

Sincerely,
The {{name}} Team.
